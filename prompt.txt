\section*{Dirac equation}

\begin{minipage}[t]{0.72\textwidth}
\noindent\textbf{A relativistic wave equation for spin-\(\tfrac{1}{2}\) particles
such as the electron.}

\medskip
Formal: \( (i\hbar\,\gamma^\mu \partial_\mu - mc)\,\psi = 0 \)

\medskip
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item What: First‑order Lorentz‑covariant equation for fermions.
  \item Why: Unifies spin and relativity; predicts antiparticles.
  \item Conventions: metric \( (+,-,-,-) \); keep \(\hbar,c\) explicit here.
\end{itemize}
